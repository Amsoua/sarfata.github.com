Biography

Grown up in France with a passion for computers and the Internet, Thomas was barely an intern during the first Internet bubble. Since graduating, Thomas has taken part in several entrepreneurship adventures, the latest as CTO of Backelite for six years.

Very curious of all technical things, Thomas has built robots, enjoyed long distance amateur radio, kernel drivers hacking and security testing. When not in front of a computer, he sails, takes pictures or try to understand Yoga.

In 2012, Thomas moved to the bay area for new adventures.
